Breaded BBQ Chicken

Description

Breaded chicken is delicious, especially when paired with a good bbq sauce. This chicken is first breaded and baked, then covered in a bbq sauce and baked again as a glaze.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast, thighs, or tenders
  • 2-3 eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • BBQ sauce
  • Spices and seasonings (optional)
