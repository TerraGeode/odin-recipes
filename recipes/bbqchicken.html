Breaded BBQ Chicken

Description

Breaded chicken is delicious, especially when paired with a good bbq sauce. This chicken is first breaded and baked, then covered in a bbq sauce and baked again as a glaze.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast, thighs, or tenders
  • 2-3 eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • BBQ sauce
  • Spices and seasonings (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. If using breast or thighs, slice into strips
  3. In one bowl, add bread crumbs and dry spices and seasonings if any
  4. In another bowl, beat the eggs until combined, one egg for every 2 breast, 3 thighs, or 6 strips
  5. For each strip, dip into eggs, shake off excess, coat in bread crumbs, then repeat with eggs and bread crumbs
  6. Lay the strips out on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 25 minutes
  7. Coat each strip in bbq sauce, if desired, sprinkle dry spices and seasonings
  8. Bake for another 10 minutes, allow to cool, and enjoy
