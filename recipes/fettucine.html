Fettucine Alfredo

Description

Fettucine alfredo makes for a wonderful dinner dish, just about any night, both with or without chicken or other additions.

Ingredients

  • Fettucine
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Garlic (garlic powder as a substitute)
  • Parmesean (grated)
  • Spices and other seasonings (optional)
  • Chicken (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to boiling
  2. Simmer fettucine in the boiling water for 8 minutes
  3. If using chicken, heat a pot to medium high heat, and cook until white and cooked through
  4. Strain water out of fettucine, and return to pot
  5. Add heavy cream to fettucine, as well as parmesean, garlic, and other spices and seasoning
  6. Mix until combined
  7. If using chicken, add chicken and mix until combine, and stir and heat until boiling
  8. Allow to cool, serve and enjoy.
