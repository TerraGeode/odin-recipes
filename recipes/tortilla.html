Tortillas

Description

Tortillas are a delicious part of any meal, especially if they are home made. In order to get the most of out these tortillas, be sure to eat them fresh. The dough is storeable, however do keep it refrigerated in a sealed container.

Ingredients

  • 200 g flour
  • 130 g warm water
  • 20 g butter (lard, oil, or shortening can be used as substitutes)
  • Salt
  • extra flour for dusting

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the flour, butter, and 2 pinches of salt.
  2. Heat the water as hot as you can handle if kneading/mixing by hand.
  3. Add the water to the flour mixture. Mix and knead until smooth.
  4. Divide the dough into 30 - 40 g balls.
  5. Heat a pan or griddle to medium high to high heat
  6. Using extra flour for dusting, roll out each dough ball
  7. Cook each tortilla for 30 to 40 seconds in each side.
